Festival Day

You are more than welcomed to join International Club in our Italiano Festival. In this day, Italian food and drinks will be provided so you can enjoy a touch of Italian gastronomy such as traditional pasta, spaghetti... Besides, joining the fun and interesting traditional games can brings you exciting experiences. For entertainment, you can enjoy the Italian musical and dancing performance from University of Social Science and Humanity's students. Lastly, our sponsor - Ducati, will have a booth showcasing their motorbikes, this probably sounds interesting to you guys. So come to our Italiano Festival, we are giving you a chance to get to know more about this boot-shaped country and having fun. Remember the date December 4th,2014!

Date: Thursday week 8, 4/12/2014 - starts at 11am.
Location: Grass field building 9.

Booths

Food & Drink

With a long history, the Italian gastronomy has always been famous among the fellow European countries. You may have heard of some of the world-renowned signature dishes of Italian cuisine like pasta, tiramisu and risotto. Now you can get a taste of a delightful and exotic cuisine from an Italian restaurant.

Culture & Art

Activities

We will prepare some Italian traditional games. They have existed for a long time and many generations of Italians have enjoyed them. You may want to join in these fun and refreshing games with your friends.

Musical Performance

Our partner, the students from the Italian major of University of Social Sciences and Humanities will provide you with many interesting and surprising facts about the Italian culture and language. They will also perform some Italian folk songs at the end of the Festival. Besides, we will also prepare a flashmob with Italian folk dance together. Feel free to join us!

Ducati's Booth

Founded in 1926, Ducati has created many models of sport motorbikes for their customers. Their motorbikes have the sporty, masculine and luxurious design. And now, IC is honored to have them as our sponsor. They will participate in our festival, showcasing their new models to RMIT students. Ducati website: http://ducati.vn/
