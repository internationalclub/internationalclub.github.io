Festival Day

You are more than welcomed to join International Club in our Italiano Festival. In this day, Italian food and drinks will be provided so you can enjoy a touch of Italian gastronomy such as traditional pasta, spaghetti... Besides, joining the fun and exciting traditional games can brings you exciting experiences. For entertainment, you can enjoy the Italian musical and dancing performance from University of Social Science and Humanity's students. Lastly, our sponsor - Ducati, will have a booth showcasing their motorbikes, this probably sounds interesting to you guys. So come to our Italiano Festival, we are giving you a chance to get to know more about this boot-shaped country and having fun. Remember the date December 4th, 2014!

Date: Thursday week 8, 4/12/2014 - starts at 2pm.
Location: Grass field building 9.
